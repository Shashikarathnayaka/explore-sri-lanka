Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting Sri Lanka

Visa

Most visitors can obtain an ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) online before arrival. Valid for 30 days, extendable up to 6 months.

Best Time to Visit

West & south coast: December–March. East coast & Cultural Triangle: May–September. Avurudu (New Year) in April is a wonderful cultural experience.

Currency

Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs are widely available. US Dollars and Euros are accepted at most tourist sites.

Getting Around

Scenic train rides through the Hill Country are a must. Tuk-tuks for short distances, buses are economical for long routes.

Dress Code

Cover shoulders and knees when visiting temples. Bring light, breathable clothing for the coast and warmer layers for the hills.

Food

Try rice & curry, kottu roti, hoppers, and fresh seafood. Street food is delicious but stick to busy stalls for safety.
